Help - Issue Sectors

Find out which department is responsible for different types of public issues in India, along with their contact numbers.

🛣️ Infrastructure Issues

  • Potholes & Road Damage: Public Works Department (PWD) — 1800-425-1100
  • Streetlight Problems: Municipal Corporation's Electrical Department — 1912
  • Illegal Constructions: Urban Development Authority — 1800-11-3355

💧 Water & Sanitation

  • Water Leakage: Water Supply and Sewerage Board (WSSB) — 1916
  • Drainage Blockage: Municipal Corporation Sanitation Department — 155303
  • Unclean Public Areas: Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) — 1800-11-1969

⚡ Electricity & Power

  • Power Outages: State Electricity Board (SEB) — 1912
  • Damaged Power Lines: Electricity Distribution Company (Discom) — 1800-180-0104
  • Streetlight Faults: Municipal Electrical Department — 1800-233-4455

🚔 Law & Order

  • Illegal Activities: Local Police Station — 100
  • Women Safety: Women's Helpline — 1091
  • Child Labour Reports: Childline — 1098

🚮 Waste Management

  • Garbage Collection Issues: Solid Waste Management Department — 1800-345-6789
  • Hazardous Waste Disposal: Pollution Control Board — 1800-233-3345
  • Recycling Concerns: Municipal Environmental Department — 1800-11-0391

🌿 Environmental Concerns

  • Tree Cutting: Forest Department — 1926
  • Air & Water Pollution: State Pollution Control Board (SPCB) — 1800-233-5005
  • Wildlife Protection: Wildlife Crime Control Bureau (WCCB) — 1800-11-7776
